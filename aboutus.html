About Us


Apeirogon Ventures Pvt. Ltd. is the creator of PrepSquare a self-assessment tool for competitive exam test-takers. We're bringing analytics and great content to students that is focused towards assessment & revision. For JEE preparation we bring independent teachers online and connect them directly with students, allowing teachers to monetize their skills directly. For students we provide a product geared to help them make the most of the last 30-60 days of their prep. Our mission is to break the another-brick-in-the-wall type of education that we receive today, and provide students with personalized tools and content to make the most of their prep.

We are a group of IIT / IIM graduates with experience in teaching, consulting and technology

We are backed by India's biggest media house through Times Internet Limited
