What Does PrepQuotient Represent

The PrepQuotient is a measurement of the following

Carefully graded problem sets

Prepsquare has problem sets for different stages and levels of preparation: Fundamental, CBSE, JEE Mains and JEE Advanced. Each problem set is designed by a teacher to teach you all the concepts they feel are the most important.

Optional test mode

Practice the questions one at a time in practice mode; or take the test mode in which your test strategy is assessed. Each problem set has roughly 20-25 questions which has been calibrated to be a 1hour test.

Award winning experienced faculty

PrepSquare gives you access to the best teachers from Delhi, Kota, Mumbai and Chandigarh for engineering preparation. Between them, our faculty partners have produced hundreds of under-50 AIRs over the last 20 years. Never before has a student had access to so many great teachers on one platform.

Video solutions to each question

PrepSquare gives you a video solution for each question, created by the same faculty who made the test. The focus of the video is to explain the key concepts and tips and tricks that you can use for the exam. You will be shown only those video segments that you did wrong or that the teacher thinks are important so you do not waste time.

Performance assessment

We have created a very advanced system to assess your performance. Understand what part of trigonometry or organic chemistry you are weak in, and then spend your time attempting problems of an appropriate level.

Direct recommendations

PrepSquare has smart algorithms to figure out how best to plan out your study schedule. Our system provides you with recommendations on what problem set is most apt for you, and how you can improve your exam taking strategy.
